A Prison (or Jail as we commonly refer to it) is any placed used permanently or temporally under the general or special orders of the State Government for the detention of prisoners. This web-site has been setup to provide information about the jail setup of the state of Madhya Pradesh in India. We would appreciate feedback if you would take time to fill in our feedback form.
The main activities are Custodial Security of Prisoners, Maintenance, Professional Training and Moral Education for the change criminal attitude
